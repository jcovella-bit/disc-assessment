DISC Behavioral Assessment
How to Complete This Assessment
Read these instructions carefully before proceeding.
1
You will see 25 groups of four words. Each group contains descriptors that reflect different behavioral tendencies.
2
For each group, click MOST under the word that is most like you in your typical work environment. Then click LEAST under the word that is least like you.
3
You must select exactly one MOST and one LEAST per group — and they cannot be the same word. All 25 groups must be completed before submitting.
4
Answer instinctively — there are no right or wrong answers. Choose based on your natural tendencies, not who you think you should be.
Example
Decisive
MOST ✓
Enthusiastic
Patient
LEAST ✓
Analytical

In this example, the respondent selected "Decisive" as most like them and "Patient" as least like them.
